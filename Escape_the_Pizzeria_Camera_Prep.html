Sc 2 INT. PIZZERIA JAIL CELL

A shoe lace gets pulled out from a boot belonging to Britney. She passes it to Shiloh as does Judah an orange one, Phineas his laces, Liam his. Shiloh ties them all together to her boot laces connected to a single boot of hers. Creating a shoe on a long shoe-string rope. She takes it and twirls it like a stone in a sling-shot and tosses it outside the jail cell. It lands behind a big stack of pizza boxes on the ground. She pulls it back towards her, bringing back the stack of pizzas with it. The security camera turret pans over the pizza boxes. Shiloh stops from pulling the pizza boxes and it freezes as the camera and its red laser passes over them, now still ...it pans back to the other side of the room and they spring back into action.

Britney and Phineas start rapidly opening and searching through the pizza boxes. Shiloh tosses the shoe back out and pulls the wooden cheese stool into their cell. Liam grabs it and breaks a leg off. Britney finds a doughie pizza and hands it to Liam who wraps it around two of the bars and uses the leg from the stool to create turn-kit. He twists his turn-kit until the two bars are together, creating a big enough gap for Judah who slips out!

The security camera is turning back towards Judah as he runs for the table! He grabs the pizza slicer on the table, hops up on it and slices the exposed wire of the camera with the pizza slicer. It shuts down. The others slip out of the jail cell. Liam heads for the door as Judah rips the camera off of the wall and tosses it to Liam who catches it and uses it as a hammer to bang the two hinges off of the door, which Shiloh opens. They all pause, happy with their successfully executed plan. They look out at where they are. Judah is obscured from being behind Phineas and Liam.

JUDAH

I can't see, I can't see!

He pushes through them, looks up at their height. He exits off and comes back in with an extra stool from the corner. He puts it down to get over their shoulders.
