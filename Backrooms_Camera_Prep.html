Script

What in the world... Where am I?

Baby cut: We now see a new clip as he's seen a ladder that leads up into a hole. He zooms in on what's down the hole — it's more hallways. He hears a noise off camera, whips to see THE MONSTER walk past a hallway further away. He panics and runs.

GERALD (O.S.) (CONT'D)

What was that?! There's something in here!

INT. Backrooms
10:00–11:30  |  MICAH
You're doomed forever

Script

Shiloh stands in the middle of a group of raelo citizens of a modern-day farmville-type raelo. All eyes are on her.

SHILOH

You'll reroute the water into Mayor Lewis' collection systems, providing irrigation to both parties.

PEOPLE

Ay!

They all clap and bustle about, happy with the plan. She turns, happy with herself, to be face to face with LIAM.

SHILOH

Liam? How'd you get here?

LIAM

Better question; Why am I here? (then) I want to be a part of your agency.

Shiloh makes a quizzical face.

SHILOH

You rule your battle royale world, why would you want to give that up?

LIAM

My sister Ashley has that place under control. I'd rather help you travel the whole raeloverse and help people. I want to work with you.

SHILOH

Who says I want you?

Liam smiles.

LIAM

You want me.

He's right. Shiloh realizes it.

She stops herself from saying 'Not true.' He grins.

Elijah pops in between them.

ELIJAH

Shiloh! Liam? What are you doing here? (beat) Have either of you seen a small child with a bag of explosives?

They both turn and look to him.

ELIJAH (CONT'D)

I mean... she definitely doesn't have explosives.

They both point in the same direction.

ELIJAH (CONT'D)

Thanks. Jocelyn!

He runs off.

LIAM

You know I'm useful.

SHILOH

(surprised) Liam, I can't do this right now.

She turns and walks away about her business. He follows after.

Script

They move forward to find a dumpster sitting in a room, completely out of place.

JOCELYN

Like a pocket reality we somehow popped into?

JOCELYN

Ha. It's GTA6.

SHILOH

I don't know. But I'm going to figure it out.

LIAM

There's something really wrong about this place.

Across the room, they see more scribbles — a picture of the spider-looking creatures with a human-to-scale next to it, showing how giant these spider creatures are, named 'Crawlers'. Shiloh also sees scribbles saying: 'I just feel LOST'.

SHILOH

(reading) I just feel lost...

Jocelyn picks up a loose sock from near the dumpster.

JOCELYN

Wow! A left sock! I wonder if this is where all our left socks disappear to? That makes so much sense!

ELIJAH

How do you know that's a left sock?

JOCELYN

Oh my gosh, you don't know how to tell the difference between your left and right sock? No wonder you're walking funny.

She grabs a second sock and holds them up.

JOCELYN (CONT'D)

You see, this one is making an L and this one isn't.

She exits. Elijah suddenly looks bewildered — is he? He follows after her.

Shiloh stops — the tile in front of her is gone and it's a dark hole. She looks down into it; it descends so deep until it gets dark, no bottom in sight. She pulls off a clip from the straps on her pants and drops it down. We hear the sound of it falling, but no clank of it hitting the bottom. She looks up and walks around the hole. The shadow of the creature from the opening is on the wall.

Script

They round a new corner and are jaw-dropped. We see what they're approaching — what looks like a gap in the floor, and then an industrial concrete hallway in front.

LIAM

Is that a new hallway?

ELIJAH

How big is this place?

SHILOH

It's like it's copying the earth, but without any idea of how it works.

ELIJAH

I feel like a rat in a maze but there's no cheese.

JOCELYN

I would like cheese.

ELIJAH

There's no cheese.

LIAM

There's a ledge here.

They see a ledge of yellow flooring that runs along the wall to a different hallway. They shimmy out onto it. Jocelyn and Elijah are clearly not doing well. Elijah looks to a cubicle to see an obscure silhouette of a partygoer shape that stares at them, then runs off. Elijah and Jocelyn are frozen, wide-eyed. They turn to each other.

ELIJAH

Our imaginations are making us see things.

JOCELYN

We have got to calm down.

ELIJAH

I'd rather be at the dentist.

JOCELYN

I'd rather be at school. (Beat. Laughing) No, I wouldn't.

Sc 10
INT. Backrooms
3:50–4:20  |  MICAH
Chill of a thousand souls

Script

They walk through a long yellow hallway, getting near the end. Shiloh is deep in thought.

LIAM

Have you thought about my question?

Shiloh looks to him, taken aback.

SHILOH

I've thought a lot about it.

She looks at where they are.

SHILOH (CONT'D)

Someone had to have made this place, right? If someone did make it... Why? They clearly had no idea the purpose or how rooms work.

LIAM

How do you know someone made this place? How do you know it doesn't just exist?

SHILOH

I don't. (beat) Yet.

LIAM

You know, that look you get when you're determined is really cute.

Shiloh looks at him — she clearly does like this guy. We see Liam, and now Elijah behind him, having heard that, slowly turning his head to Liam with murder in his eyes.

ELIJAH

What did you say?

LIAM

You heard me.

ELIJAH

OH! You're done! You're so done!

Elijah furiously pats down his belt, then his pockets, then his boots.

ELIJAH (CONT'D)

Oh!! Gosh dang it! This place took my taser.

Jocelyn looks off with wide eyes, towards where they just came from, down the long hallway stretching before them with an emergency industrial door halfway open. At the end of the hallway, a silhouette of THE MONSTER rounds the corner and stares at them quizzically. It's distant and blurry — barely more than a large shape.

JOCELYN

Guys?!

They all look to her, then down the hallway. The thing stops and they stare, hearts racing. It takes off running full speed at them.

ELIJAH

It's running at us.

SHILOH

Run!!

Jocelyn takes off running down one of the paths, then Elijah. Shiloh goes to run, when she turns and sees Liam not running, but thinking. Liam quickly weighs his options, then takes off full sprinting towards the monster. Shiloh turns back to him.

SHILOH (CONT'D)

Liam!

Liam sprints towards the monster as we see the blurry shape getting closer — in full gallop on all fours. Shiloh is torn between running and watching Liam. Elijah and Jocelyn run down their path, when they stop midway — the floor is just gone into a weird chasm. They barely stop themselves.

ELIJAH

(hollering back to Shiloh) It's a dead end!

Shiloh hears this and looks back to Liam as we cut between him and the monster getting closer. He reaches the door first and shuts it closed, then dead bolts it. He breathes and looks back to Shiloh.

LIAM

You're welcome. (alt: he winks)

Shiloh realizes her plan would've failed. Elijah and Jocelyn arrive back to Shiloh.

ELIJAH

(to Liam) Alright, you can stay for exactly however long it takes to get us out of here... Then you're gone.

JOCELYN

How are you going to beat him up, he's like twice your size?

ELIJAH

Shh. I haven't figured that part out yet.

LIAM

(to Shiloh) You need me on your team. You know it.

He reacts to little squirts of water hitting his face. He looks as we cut wide to reveal Elijah with a tiny water blaster, squirting Liam with it.

LIAM (CONT'D)

Whatcha doin'?

ELIJAH

I found a mini squirt blaster in my boot! Aha!

Liam rips it out of Elijah's hand and tosses it off. Elijah stares at his empty hand, dumb-founded.

ELIJAH (CONT'D)

Well, that's never happened before.
